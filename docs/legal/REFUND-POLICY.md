# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026
**Product:** Relix (relix.sh)

---

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with your Relix subscription, you can get a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or any renewal. No questions asked.

This applies to all paid tiers: Plus, Pro, and Team.

---

## Pro-Rata Refunds for Annual Plans

If you cancel an annual subscription after the 14-day window, you can request a pro-rata refund for the unused months remaining on your term.

**Example:** You purchase an annual Plus plan ($49.90/year). After 4 months, you decide to cancel. You're eligible for a refund of approximately $33.27 (8 remaining months out of 12).

---

## Monthly Plans

Monthly subscriptions cancelled after the 14-day window are not eligible for a refund. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period, and you will not be charged again.

---

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Email** support@shadowscale.dev with the subject line "Refund Request"
2. **Include** the email address associated with your Relix account
3. We'll confirm your refund within 1 business day

That's it. No forms to fill out, no hoops to jump through.

---

## Processing Time

Refunds are processed within **5-10 business days** to your original payment method via Stripe. The exact timing depends on your bank or card issuer.

---

## Exceptions

- Refunds are not available for the Free tier (there's nothing to refund).
- Chargebacks filed before contacting us may delay resolution. Please reach out to us first — we'll make it right.
- If your account was terminated for violating our Terms of Service, refund eligibility is determined on a case-by-case basis.
